# Computer Tear Down

## Goals

* Develop some comfort with taking computers apart

* Learn about proper safety procedures


## Materials

* Screw Drivers

* Bins

* Computers!

## Lesson

* Safety

  * Power down the device

    * Wait

    * Capacitors

  * Remove batteries

  * Static Discharge

  * Handle with care! - Don't cause shorts

  * Move slowly - Don't force anything

* Take em apart

* Identify

  * RAM

  * Harddrive

  * CPU

  * PSU

  * Cooler
