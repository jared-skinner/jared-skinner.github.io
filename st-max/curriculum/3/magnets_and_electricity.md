## Goals
* Understand what electricity is

## Vocabulary
* Magnet
* Electricity
* Current
* AC/DC

## Materials
* 9-volt batteries
* nails
* copper wire
* compasses
* paper clips

## Lesson

* Building an electro-magnet (20 mins)
